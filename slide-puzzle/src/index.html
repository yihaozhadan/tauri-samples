Slide Puzzle

How to Play

  • Goal: Arrange the numbers in order from 1 to N with the empty space at the end.
  • Movement: Click any tile adjacent to the empty space to move it.
  • Win: The puzzle is solved when all numbers are in ascending order.
Difficulty Levels:
  • Easy: 3×3 grid (8 tiles)
  • Medium: 4×4 grid (15 tiles)
  • Hard: 5×5 grid (24 tiles)
